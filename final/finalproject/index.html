House of Cats

There are too many cats in this house and they're bursting with energy. Help us get rid of some of their energy and choose which cat you want to play with to compete against your friend.

Rules of Play

  1. Pick two cards. The total will be added to your score and you pick again.
  2. However, if you draw one "cat eye," your turn is over.
  3. If both cards are "cat eyes," your turn is over and your score zeroes out.
  4. After each turn, you can either draw again or pass if you're worried you might draw two “cat eyes.”
  5. The first player to 30 points or more wins!
