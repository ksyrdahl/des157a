House of Cats

There are too many cats in this house and they're bursting with energy. Help us get rid of some of their energy and choose which cat you want to play with. To avoid absolute chaos you can only play with two cats at a time. The cat whose turn it is picks two cards. The total of the cards is added to the current cats' score, unless either card comes up as a "cat eyes." If this happens, this cat's turn is over, and it is the other cats' turn. After each roll, the current cat can either pick a card again (assuming a "one" was not drawn), or if the current cat feels tired, they can pass to the other player. The first player to get 30 points or higher wins.

Oh, and if you roll two "cat eyes", your current score gets zeroed out. So don't do that.
