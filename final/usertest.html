Test Summary

I had three different people test my game without knowing anything about it. I asked them to "read the game rules," "Play one game all the way through playing for both player 1 aand player 2," "Start a new game, and click the help button," and "Quit the game." Below is the feedback that I got from watching their interactions.

1

Observation

I noticed that my rules are too long. I was struggling with explaining the information in a clear and concise way and it took each user a long time to read the rules.

Solution

Someone suggested that I number them which I think is a good way to make it less overwhelming. Someone also suggested that I bold the important information.

2

Observation

While picking an avatar, something I noticed is that some people went to try and click the image of the cats instead of the name button.

Solution

I think I will make it so you can click the name button and the image to move on to the next page instead of just the name button.

3

Observation

There were some readability issues because of the background that moves. The white sections of the background were easier to read than the pink ones.

Solution

I might add a transparent white background behind the rules in the intro to make it more readable.
